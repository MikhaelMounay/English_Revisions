Cycle 1

big VS large

big can refer to the physical size of an object, but it also means that something is serious or important, while large only refers to the physical size.

search VS search for VS do research

  • search يبحث في
  • search for يبحث عن
  • do research about / on يعمل بحث علمي

[singular or plural]

  • not only ... but also ... → [ + plural]
  • both ... and ... → [ + plural]
  • the +adj. → [ + plural]
  • ... as well as ... → [singular / plural - على حسب أول subject]
  • neither ... nor ... → [singular / plural - على حسب اللى بعد nor]
  • each (one) of ... → [singular]
  • every ... → [singular]

CPR (Cardiopulmonary resuscitation)

  • do CPR
  • perform CPR
  • carry out CPR

feast VS festival

  • feast - احتفال (related to religion)
  • festival - احتفال عادي

hygiene VS sanitation

  • hygiene - النظافة الشخصية
  • sanitation - النظافة العامة

Modal verbs

  • shall → offer & suggest
  • should → any advice
  • must → strong advice
  • have (got) to → orders & rules
  • should have +p.p → blaming - التوبيخ

Strongest to Weakest orders:

  1. have to
  1. must
  1. ought to
  1. should

"need" as a modal verb

  • needn't have +p.p → مش لازم بس اتعملت
  • didn't need to → مش لازم ومتعملتش

dialogue VS conversation

  • dialogue → written better
  • conversation → spoken better

put (someone) through

    "by the age of 30"

means VS news

  • means (countable)

arbiter VS referee

  • arbiter → الحكم بين

    "the military acted as arbiter of conflicts between political groups"

  • referee → حكم (في مباراة مثلا)

"no article" + noun

  • go to bed
  • go to work
  • on duty
  • go to school

verbs: forget, regret & remember

  • forget to +inf. → نسيت أن أفعل شيء

    "I forgot to post the letter."

  • forget +ing. → أنسى أنني فعلت شيء

    "I will never forget winning my first national award."

past simple sentences

** a past simple sentence cannot be written without limiting time keyword.

"I forgot to buy the groceries" ❌

"I forgot to buy the groceries yesterday" ✅
